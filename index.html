Trezor.io/Start® | Starting Up Your Device - Trezor®

Welcome to Trezor.io/Start®, the official site to set up your Trezor® Hardware Wallet. Whether you're new to cryptocurrency or an experienced user, this guide will help you get started quickly and securely with your Trezor wallet.

Step 1: Connect Your Trezor Device

Begin by unboxing your Trezor wallet and connecting it to your computer using the provided USB cable. Once connected, visit Trezor.io/Start® and download the Trezor Suite App, available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. This app is the essential tool for managing your Trezor device and safeguarding your cryptocurrency.

Step 2: Set Up Your Wallet

After installing the Trezor Suite App, follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your Trezor wallet. This process includes setting up a PIN and generating a recovery seed, which acts as your backup in case your device is lost or damaged. Be sure to store the recovery seed in a safe location.

Step 3: Manage Your Cryptocurrency

Once your Trezor is set up, you can securely manage your digital assets. The Trezor Suite App allows you to monitor transactions, track balances, and update security settings to keep your crypto safe.

Why Use Trezor?

The Trezor® Hardware Wallet offers unmatched security by keeping your private keys offline, protecting your assets from online threats. Start today and safeguard your cryptocurrency with Trezor.
